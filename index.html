How it works
This DEX uses a fixed rate of 1 ETH = 1,000 FUN with a 0.3% swap fee. ETH→FUN sends ETH to the GameHub contract (which accepts it via its receive() function). FUN→ETH approves and transfers FUN tokens back to the contract. All transactions are on-chain on Arbitrum Sepolia.

Note: This is a testnet DEX. Tokens and ETH have no real value.
